Our Services

Import Customs Clearance

L.J Palma Customs Brokerage primary duty is to comply with all the requirements, rules and regulation implemented by the Bureau of Customs to make sure a hassle-free and legal transactions. We can immediately classify and acquire tariff rates and computation of duties and taxes at the lowest rate possible. We guarantee 1-3 days delivery lead time from the date of container discharge from the vessel, with constant supervision and update with day-to-day status up to the delivery of the cargoes as well as the timely and accurate billing services.

Export Customs Clearance

A precise and efficient coordination with the appropriate government and private institutions to facilitate right away the documentation and cargo processing. Continuous monitoring of cargo movement up to departure for loading furthermore issuance of export documentation copies and shipper's billing.
